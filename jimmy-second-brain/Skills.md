# Skills

These are your installed Claude skills. Just say the trigger words in Cowork and Claude will run the skill automatically — no commands to memorize.

---

## 🌅 Good Morning
**Trigger:** Say "good morning", "let's get to work", or "start my day"

Reads your vault, recaps what you've been working on from recent logs, and recommends the most important thing to tackle first. Run this at the start of every session.

---

## 🌙 End of Day
**Trigger:** Say "/eod", "wrap up", "end of day", or "that's it for today"

Logs the session — what you worked on, what's still open, any decisions made. Saves a handoff note so the next Claude session picks up right where you left off.

---

## 📁 New Project
**Trigger:** Say "new project", "start a project", or "create a project"

Asks you a few questions about your project, then creates a fully set up project folder with a Project Overview and Principles Found file — pre-filled with your answers and ready to work from immediately.

---

## ⚙️ Setup
**Trigger:** Say "set up my second brain" *(first time only)*

Personalizes the vault with your name and a description of what you're building. Only needed once when you first open a new vault.
