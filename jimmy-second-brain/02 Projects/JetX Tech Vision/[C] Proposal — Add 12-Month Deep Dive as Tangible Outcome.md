# Proposal — Add "12-Month Deep Dive" as a Tangible Outcome

## Ask

> - **12-Month Deep Dive** — quarter-by-quarter tactical plan for the next year, written for an internal audience, milestone-driven, no marketing language; supports the 2026-05-16/17 workshop.

Place it immediately above the existing `- Timeline / roadmap for the next 3 years` bullet, since the 12-month plan is the high-fidelity input that the 3-year roadmap is built on.

## Why

Jimmy is hosting an internal workshop the weekend of **2026-05-16/17** and needs a 12-month deep-dive document to support his high-level presentations. The existing "3-year roadmap" outcome doesn't fit the need:

- **Different fidelity** — the 12-month deep dive is quarter-by-quarter milestones with owners and dependencies; the 3-year roadmap is directional.
- **Different audience** — the 12-month deep dive is internal-team tactical; the 3-year roadmap is broader (likely investor/board-shaped).
- **Different urgency** — the 12-month has a hard deadline this weekend; the 3-year is open-ended.

Treating them as one outcome would either delay the workshop or dilute the 3-year roadmap. Splitting them is cheaper and more honest about the actual artifacts being produced.
